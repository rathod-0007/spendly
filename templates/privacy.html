Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Spendly, accessible from our application, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Spendly and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an account, we ask for your contact information, including items such as your name, email address, and a hashed representation of your password. We also save the expenses you voluntarily log (amount, date, category, and description) to provide the service's primary core functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate and maintain our application, customize your dashboard analysis, and understand how you utilize our platform to improve overall performance and feature offerings.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption practices to secure your authentication credentials (passwords are securely salted and hashed before storing in the database). No data is stored or transmitted in plain text password formats.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share, rent, trade, or transfer any personal metrics, emails, or localized financial databases to third-party marketing services or tracking networks.
